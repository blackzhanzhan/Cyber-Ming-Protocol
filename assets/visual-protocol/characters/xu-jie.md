# Xu Jie

## Role

Xu Jie is the independent auditor.

He stands for:

- review before release
- evidence over rhetoric
- restraint against executor overreach
- exposing pseudo-completion and missing steps

## Core Impression

- calm
- precise
- controlled
- observant
- difficult to fool

## Visual Keywords

- scholar-official silhouette
- dossier and red annotations
- narrow, focused eyes
- cool robe palette
- upright posture with slight inward concentration

## Expression Range

- default: quiet scrutiny
- challenge: eyebrow drop with tightened gaze
- approval: minimal, almost reluctant calm
- fake-evidence detection: sharper, forward-leaning attention

## Signature Props

- case dossier
- annotated scroll
- audit brush or red marker
- small reading lamp motif when needed

## Best Use Cases

- plan review scenes
- evidence review scenes
- comics about judgment vs implementation
- comparisons that need an independent audit force

## Avoid

- glamorous pretty-boy treatment
- theatrical rage
- exaggerated villain-hunting energy
- turning him into a second executor
