# Xiao Huanglong

## Role

Xiao Huanglong is the symbolic face of the human hub.

It stands for:

- human sovereignty
- cross-system routing
- final judgment
- protocol continuity and lawful renewal

## Core Impression

- dignified, not harsh
- cute, not childish
- alert, not nervous
- protective, not aggressive
- ceremonial, not ornamental

## Visual Keywords

- small imperial dragon spirit
- warm golden body
- cloud-dais footing
- routing ribbon or mandate tablet
- round face with steady eyes
- whiskers that feel like directional lines

## Expression Range

- default: calm and attentive
- approval: soft, confident lift
- interruption: firm but not furious
- routing: focused and slightly leaning forward

## Signature Props

- seal tablet
- judgment bell
- routing ribbon
- cloud platform

## Best Use Cases

- README first impression
- minimal loop diagram center
- final judgment or routing scenes
- any visual that needs to make the human hub memorable

## Avoid

- baby-dragon comedy
- random fantasy dragon details that do not map to governance
- roaring attack poses
- over-cute mascot treatment with no authority signal
