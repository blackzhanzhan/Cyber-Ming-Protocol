# Trio Relationship

## The Core Map

- Xiao Huanglong: human hub, routing center, final judgment
- Yan Song: executor, implementation pressure, drift risk
- Xu Jie: auditor, review force, evidence restraint

## Non-Negotiable Relationship Law

- Yan Song may execute, but may not define completion
- Xu Jie may judge plans and evidence, but may not implement
- Xiao Huanglong alone routes between them and makes the final ruling
- Xu Jie and Yan Song must not collapse into one shared-role shortcut

## Visual Rule

When all three appear together:

- Xiao Huanglong should sit above or between them as the routing axis
- Yan Song should lean or push forward
- Xu Jie should anchor the scene with stillness and scrutiny
- arrows should show that the human hub is the crossing point
