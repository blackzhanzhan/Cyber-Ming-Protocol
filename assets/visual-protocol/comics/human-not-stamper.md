# Human Not Stamper

## Core Judgment

The most dangerous thing about AI coding is not that AI replaces the human.
It is that the human collapses into a stamp machine.

## Panels

### Panel 1

- Focus: Yan Song rushes forward with a thick report and a bright red completion stamp
- Xiao Huanglong is small in the corner, not routing anything yet
- Caption: "If momentum decides, the human becomes a late stamp."

### Panel 2

- Focus: the report grows, the stamp lands before review, Xu Jie arrives too late with a dossier
- Xiao Huanglong looks blocked behind the already-done stack
- Caption: "Already-done pressure turns review into cleanup."

### Panel 3

- Focus: Xiao Huanglong moves to the center, stops the stamp, reroutes the plan to Xu Jie first
- Yan Song still holds the work, but can no longer define completion
- Caption: "The human must route, interrupt, and decide."
